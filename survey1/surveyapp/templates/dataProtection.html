Data Protection Information

Anonymity and Confidentiality:
The study is hosted on a Virtual Private Server located in a secure data center in Amsterdam.
The Principal Researcher at University College London (UCL) owns the data and has complete control over it.
Your data will be recorded electronically and stored securely.
All information that is collected from you will be kept strictly confidential, stored in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998 and the General Data Protection Regulation will not be shared with anyone else.
Your responses will not be linked to your identity and will therefore be anonymous.
Anonymised and group data may be published in journals or presented at conferences.
We will never share data that could identify you.

Ethics:
This research project was reviewed and exempted from approval by the Ethics Committee at the Department of Security Crime Science at University College London (UCL).

Your right to withdraw:
It is up to you to decide whether to take part or not; choosing not to take part will not disadvantage you, or affect your rights, in any way.
If you decide to take part, you are free to withdraw at any time and without giving a reason.
