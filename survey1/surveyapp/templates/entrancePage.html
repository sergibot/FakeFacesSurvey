Survey Introduction Page

This survey concerns an Artificial Intelligence technology which creates images of faces of people who don't otherwise exist.


The faces in these AI-generated images do not belong to existing humans.

From lots of examples of images of human faces, the AI technology has learned what images of human faces look like.

The AI technology has then created new images,
each of which seems to portray a human face.





We would like to test people's accuracy at telling apart
images generated by this AI technology from
images of existing human faces.
