Introductory Help


Below are examples of ten "tell-tale" features that the AI-generated images tend to exhibit.

Again, these images feature the faces of people who do not exist.

The features are divided into three categories:  Background, Asymmetry, and Abnormalities.



Note that perhaps none of these features will appear on a given AI-generated image.

Note also that these are not the only features that can be used to distinguish the AI-generated images.

The features are a guide; it is important to also apply an element of common sense.



In the main question sequence, you will be tested on your ability to distinguish these images from images of faces of people who do exist.

You will be quizzed on your memory of these signs later.



Please study these carefully.
