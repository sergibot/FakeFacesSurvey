Task Instructions


The data you submit will be kept anonymous.


Your task is as follows:


for each image shown to you, you are asked to decide whether it is


an image of a real human face

or

an artificial image that has been created by this AI technology.



You will also be asked for the level of confidence you have in each of these answers.


After your judgment we ask you to explain a little more about your decision.

Specifically, we are interested in which elements of the image affected your decision.


In addition, you will see a copy of the image with a grid over it.

Here we ask you to click the grid-squares containing any parts of the image that affected your decision.
